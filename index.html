  • Undergraduate Journal of Peace and Conflict Studies

    The Grinnell College Student Journal of Peace and Conflict Studies includes selected papers presented at the Grinnell College Student Peace and Conflict Studies Conference, held biennially, which is open to undergraduate students only. The Journal does not consider paper submissions except those papers given in person at the Grinnell College Student Peace and Conflict Studies Conference. For information about the next conference, see: https://www.grinnell.edu/academics/centers/peace-studies/conference

    The articles collected in Pioneering Neuroscience report on original research in the field of neuroscience conducted by students in BIO 150 (Introduction to Biological Inquiry, section The Language of Neurons) and BIO 363 (Neurobiology) as part of Grinnell's distinctive inquiry-based learning program.
